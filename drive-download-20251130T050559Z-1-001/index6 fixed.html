Swords

A sword is a long-bladed weapon designed for cutting and thrusting. In real life, swords vary from lightweight dueling blades to heavy battlefield weapons, known for balance and precision.

Wands

A wand is a small, handheld rod often used in rituals, symbolism, or stage magic. It doesn’t attack physically — instead, it represents control, focus, or "channeling" energy in fictional settings.

Guns

Guns are ranged weapons that use the combustion of gunpowder to fire projectiles. They vary widely in size and function, from handguns to rifles, and are known for their power and precision in real life.
