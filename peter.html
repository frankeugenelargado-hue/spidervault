Peter B. Parker

Older, worn-out Spider-Man from another dimension, rediscovering what it means to be a hero.

“You won’t know it’s the right moment. That’s why it’s a leap of faith.”

Character Description: Peter B. Parker is an older, worn-out Spider-Man from another dimension who’s lost his confidence — and his way. Despite his flaws, he becomes an unexpected mentor to Miles and rediscovers what it means to be a hero.

Abilities

  • Wall-Crawling: Can cling to and scale walls.
  • Spider-Sense: Alerts him to nearby danger.
  • Web-Slinging & Web-Shooters:Creates webs for swinging, restraining enemies, or building temporary structures.
  • Enhanced Strength, Speed, Agility, and Reflexes.

Relationships

  • Miles Morales: Mentor and guide across the Spider-Verse.
  • Gwen Stacy: Ally and friend; works together in multiverse adventures.
  • Mary Jane: Ex-partner
  • Spider-People across Multiverse: Collaborators and friends.

Background

An older, experienced Spider-Man from another dimension. He’s a little jaded, has been divorced, and lost confidence, but he rediscovers heroism through mentoring Miles.

Fun Facts

  • First appeared in The Amazing Spider-Man #15 (1963) as Peter Parker, B version is Spider-Verse
  • Known for being a more relaxed, humorous Spider-Man
  • Often sarcastic but reliable in battle
